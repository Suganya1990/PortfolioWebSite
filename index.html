Hello There!

Learning to code and deciding to go back to school has been a difficult and arduous journey, however, I have never done something so rewarding. Now, I build multi-page websites using the fundamentals HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have since moved on to using Bootstrap, Sass, React, jQuery, API, Git, Responsive Design to build applications. I understand and have used both functional programming and object oriented programming.

What makes me a good developer is my ability to tackle and solve problems, can work within my fears, and my constant need to be challenged. While learning I have cultivated a growth mindset that will help me keep up with the industry. Since graduating I have used various resources such as FreeCodeCamp, FrontEndMentor, Udemy courses, to continue to learn. One the things I have improved that was not a main focus in school was design and design principles. I am comfortable using Figma and Canva to implement design, prototyping, and wireframing.

I am a developer because I'm inspired to be a better me today than I was yesterday. Put simply, I love to learn and these projects below are a great example of that. You can follow me and my journey on Instagram and Tik Tok.

Before I got into programming, I was in medical sales for hear.com. I used to work with seniors and clinics all across Canada. I have also worked for Facedrive, a ride share company, and have learned tons about what makes an app successful.

These days, I'm all about providing users with cohesive, meaningful, accessible, and intuitive experiences on the web.
